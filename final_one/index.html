Stay Prepared

Safety Tips for Women

Expert-backed tips to help you stay aware, confident, and safe every day.

Stay Aware of Surroundings

Avoid distractions like headphones in unfamiliar areas. Stay alert and trust your instincts.

Keep Phone Charged

Always carry a power bank. A charged phone is your lifeline in emergencies.

Travel in Groups

Whenever possible, travel with trusted friends or family, especially at night.

Share Your Route

Always inform someone you trust about your travel plans and expected arrival time.

Secure Your Digital Life

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all your accounts.

Know Emergency Numbers

Memorize local emergency numbers (100, 112, 1091 Women Helpline) and keep them accessible.
